Our Services

Custom Tooling & Fixture Design

Design and fabricate custom tooling for assembly, welding, and testing. Includes manifolds, fixturing, and low-volume production aids. Ensures precision, repeatability, and operator-friendly design.

Steel Rule Die Tooling & Alignment Systems

Design, fabricate, and optimize steel rule dies. Includes alignment systems, poka-yoke features, and precision measurement support. Both CAD and real-world validation to ensure accurate and repeatable cutting processes.

Process & Facility Layout Optimization

Plan and optimize facility layouts for efficient material flow and process control. Integrates custom piping, blast gates, workstations, and equipment to maximize throughput and safety while minimizing footprint.

Low-Cost Automation & Poka-Yoke Systems

Design and implement automation solutions for repetitive or precision tasks. Includes assembly, probe insertion, cooling, and sensor integration. Focus on affordable, reliable, and error-proof processes for operators.

Vendor Coordination & Equipment Integration

Assist with vendor selection, equipment installation, and integration into existing processes. Ensure proper communication, fit, and operational efficiency between multiple systems and suppliers.
